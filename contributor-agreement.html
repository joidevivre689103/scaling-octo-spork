1. Relationship

This agreement establishes the terms under which you ("Contributor") may submit content to Cricket Times ("Publication").

You are an independent contractor, not an employee, partner, or agent of Cricket Times. This agreement does not create an employment relationship, and you are not entitled to employee benefits of any kind.

2. Content Submissions

  • You grant Cricket Times a non-exclusive, perpetual, worldwide license to publish, display, distribute, and archive your submitted content across all platforms and formats.
  • You retain ownership of your work and may republish it elsewhere, with appropriate attribution to its original publication on Cricket Times.
  • You warrant that all submitted content is your original work and does not infringe on any third party's rights.
  • Cricket Times reserves the right to not publish your submission for any reason whatsoever. Cricket Times also reserves the right to edit your submission to meet our editorial guidelines or for any other reason whatsoever at our sole discretion.

3. Compensation

Revenue Model

Cricket Times operates on a reader-supported model. Revenue comes from one-time contributions, subscriptions (providing access to archives and other exclusive content), and advertisements. Any other sources of revenue may be considered in the future at Cricket Times' sole discretion.

Payout Structure

  • One-time contributions are pooled and distributed to contributors, after adjusting for Cricket Times costs, based on each article's share of total page views for that period.
  • Subscription revenue is pooled and distributed to contributors, after adjusting for Cricket Times costs, based on each contributor's share of archive views for that period.
  • Advertisement revenue will be used for Cricket Times expenses and will not be shared with contributors. Any excess after expenses will be treated as part of Cricket Times income.
  • Cricket Times retains a percentage of collected revenue to cover platform costs (hosting, tools, maintenance, salaries, and any other expenses). This percentage may change from time to time at Cricket Times' sole discretion.
  • Payouts are calculated and distributed monthly, subject to a minimum payout threshold. Amounts below this threshold will accumulate until the threshold is met.

No Guaranteed Payment

Compensation depends entirely on reader support. If no donations or subscriptions are received in a given period, no payouts will be made. Cricket Times makes no guarantee of any minimum payment.

4. Tax Responsibilities

You Are Responsible for Your Own Taxes

As an independent contractor, you are solely responsible for reporting and paying all applicable taxes on payments received from Cricket Times.

US-Based Contributors

  • You agree to provide a completed IRS Form W-9 before receiving any payment.
  • Cricket Times will issue you an IRS Form 1099-NEC by January 31 of the following year if you are paid $600 or more in a calendar year.

International Contributors (Non-US)

  • You agree to provide a completed IRS Form W-8BEN (or W-8BEN-E for entities) to certify your foreign status.
  • You are responsible for complying with tax laws in your country of residence.
  • Cricket Times is generally not required to withhold US taxes on payments to foreign contractors, but you should consult your own tax advisor.

Record Keeping

Cricket Times will maintain records of all payments made to you. You are responsible for maintaining your own records for tax purposes.

5. Payment Method

Payments will be made via a mutually agreed method (e.g., Wise, direct bank transfer, UPI, or other). You are responsible for any fees charged by your chosen payment provider.

6. Termination

Either party may terminate this agreement at any time with written notice. Upon termination:

  • Any unpaid amounts owed to you will be paid out (subject to the minimum threshold).
  • Cricket Times retains the license to continue displaying previously published content.

7. Modifications

Cricket Times may update this agreement from time to time. Contributors will be notified of material changes. Continued submissions after notification constitutes acceptance of the updated terms.

8. Limitation of Liability

Cricket Times is provided "as is." Cricket Times is not liable for any indirect, incidental, or consequential damages arising from this agreement or your contributions.
